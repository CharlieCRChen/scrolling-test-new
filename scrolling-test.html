Tutorial

In this test, you are going to use a scrolling technique to land either the star or a certain line to the target grey area in the center of the webpage.

If the star or the target line does not land statically in the target area, it will not activate next trial. If the target pass through the target area too quickly, it won't activate next trial either.

In the demo test, you will have several chances to get familiar with this test. Then, you will have a formal test with more trials.

Start Test

Click start and scroll to the target grey area as quickly as possible.

Attention

Now you will be told what line to scroll to, and there won't be any stars on that line.

End Test
